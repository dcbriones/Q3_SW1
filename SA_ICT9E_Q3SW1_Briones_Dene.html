Gravity Falls

Synopsis

Two twins are sent by their parents to live with their Grunkle Stan in his tourist trap known as the Mystery Shack located in Gravity Falls Oregon for an entire summer. When the two of them arrive there they quickly find the strange supernatural phenomena that constantly pop up all over the town and are on a quest to uncover its secrets with the aid of a mysterious journal that they find in the woods.

Dipper Pines

Our first protagonist and the more intellectual of the two twins, Dipper takes great interest in the supernatural and is the most keen of the two of them to uncover the mysteries of Gravity Falls. Dipper is also interested finding out who is the true author of the journal he found in the woods. Even if he gets openly mocked by his family a lot, he still cares deeply about them and never wants any harm to come to them.

Mabel Pines

Our second protagonist and the more emotional of the two twins, Mabel is more interested in finding a boyfriend during the summer and spending time with her Grunkle Stan but is still willing to help her brother in his supernatural endeavors. While she is a bit selfish at times, she cares a lot about her family much like her brother. She likes to spend her time watching TV, spending time with her friends and family, and playing with her pet pig named Waddles.

Grunkle Stan

The caretaker of the twins and owner of the Mystery Shack, Grunkle Stan is a lazy and greedy con artist that makes fake attractions for the Mystery Shack and claims to not believe in the supernatural. Though he may seem rough and lazy on the outside, he still wants nothing more than to protect his family. But who knows what secrets he could be hiding?

Soos

The loyal repairman of the Mystery Shack, Soos is oafish and lovable but always gets the job done in fixing things around the Mystery Shack. Soos cares very much about the Pines family and is enjoys helping them. He like to spend his spare time watching anime, playing video games and eating pizza and various junk foods.

Wendy

Another employee of the Mystery Shack, Wendy is a cashier that does not enjoy doing work but is still great friends with Soos and the Pines family. Even if she does not go on many adventures with the rest of the group, she still cares for them even if she is a bit lazy in doing her job. When not at work, she enjoys disobeying rules with her friends.
